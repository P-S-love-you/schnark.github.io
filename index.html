Apps for Firefox OS

This page lists my apps for Firefox OS. You can install them or (at least to some degree) use them online in any browser. All these apps are still maintained and will receive updates.

  • Calender: A calender which shows additional information for each day. (source)
  • Emoticons: A dictionary for emoticons (in German). (source)
  • Aaaa...: This app makes your phone scream when in free fall. (source)
  • Music metadata: Edit metadata of audio files. (source)
  • Sudoku Solver: An app to recognize and solve Sudokus. (source)
