Apps for Firefox OS

This page lists my apps for Firefox OS. Since they are basically web apps, you can also install and use them on other devices, including your desktop browser (at least to some degree). All these apps are still maintained and will receive updates.

Note: Due to some strange bug it may happen that you are informed about an update after you installed an app, even if there is none. Installing the update will seem to fail (the icon is replaced by a “try again” button). In this case, just restart your device, and the app should work correctly. If you understand how to properly fix this bug, please tell me about it.

  • Aaaaa...: This app makes your phone scream when in free fall. (source)
  • App Installer: This app allows you to install apps that come without their own installation code, mainly intended for testing. (source)

  • Music Editor: Write down tunes and listen to them. (source)

  • Racer: A tiny racer app. (source)
  • Firri: A feed reader for RSS and Atom feeds. (source)
  • Ruler, Protractor: Ruler and protractor. (source)

Hinweis: Ein merkwürdiger Fehler kann dazu führen, dass nach dem Installieren einer App eine Aktualisierung gemeldet wird, obwohl es keine gibt. Das Installieren der Aktualisierung scheint dann fehlzuschlagen (das Symbol wird durch eine „Nochmal versuchen“-Schaltfläche ersetzt). In diesem Fall reicht es, das Gerät neu zu starten, anschließend sollte die App korrekt funktionieren. Falls du weißt, wie der Fehler ganz behoben werden kann, teile es mir bitte mit.
